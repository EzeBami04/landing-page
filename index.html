What we do

Four services, one connected system

Most projects end up needing all four working together, from the first pipeline to the dashboard your team checks every morning.

Data Engineering

We design pipelines that move data from source to warehouse without breaking. Kafka, Airflow, dbt and Python keep your tables fresh and your team out of firefighting mode.

Data Analytics

Dashboards people actually open. We turn raw tables into Power BI and Looker reports that answer the question before anyone has to ask it.

AI Automation

Agents and workflows built with LangChain and n8n that read, write and act across the tools your team already uses, so repetitive work runs on its own.

Cloud Solutions

We build and manage your data infrastructure on Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure, from warehousing to orchestration, so it scales when your business does.
